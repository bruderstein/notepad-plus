Wrapping

Line wrapping means that lines that are longer than the actual width of the screen are wrapped (that is, continued on a new line), without actually inserting any newline characters.

To enable line wrapping, select View->Wrap or press .

To enable a visual cue where a line is wrapped as opposed to be ended with a newline character, select View->Show Symbol->Show wrap symbol. Display of where a line could wrap because its length exceeds some theshhold can be enabled and configured in both Preferences and Styler Configurator.
